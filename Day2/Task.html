ELITE CRICKET ACADEMY - DELHI TOUR

Get ready for an exhilarating new chapter in youth cricket The Delhi Cricket Tour 2025 is just around the corner this September, as Elite Cricket Academy from Melbourne gears up for an unforgettable adventure in Delhi India! They’ll be taking on the incredible Delhi Capitals Conscient Sports Cricket Academy in a thrilling bilateral series that marks start of an exciting annual cricket exchange between two top-notch academies, showcasing extraordinary talent, sportsmanship, and international camaraderie!Two energetic teams from Elite Cricket Academy—our rising stars from the Under-14 and Under-19 divisions are all set to represent Melbourne in this prestigious showdown. They'll battle it out against their Delhi counterparts in a high-octane five-match ODI series that will truly test their skills, strategies, and nerves. But this series is more than just competition; it’s an amazing opportunity for young cricketers to gain invaluable international experience, participate in two engaging workshops with top coaches, and forge lifelong friendships while embracing the global spirit of the game. This tour is ticket to experiencing the journey to IPL glory!

ELITE TECHNIQUES

Elite cricket academy techniques focus on developing the "four pillars": technical, physical, mental, and tactical skills. Key techniques include a strong batting stance with a relaxed upper body, proper head and eye position when hitting, correct weight transfer on all shots, and specialized training for bowling, fielding, and mental resilience.
