About us

Intel is an American multinational technology company that designs, manufactures, and sells computer components like CPUs and other related products. Founded in 1968, it has been a major force in the semiconductor industry, famously introducing the first commercial microprocessor in 1971 and securing a key role in the development of the personal computer. Today, the company is also a leader in producing chips for data centers, artificial intelligence (AI), graphics, and other areas

Services

Intel provides a wide range of services focused on developing and supporting computing technology, including technical enablement and platform testing for businesses, along with AI solutions, data center technologies, and networking products for various sectors. Key services include technical support, platform testing, design review services, and rental of technical equipment to help customers develop and test products with Intel technologies. The company also offers a variety of software and hardware solutions for cloud computing, AI, and edge computing.
