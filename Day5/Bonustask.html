“Colors of Life”


BLUE

The Calmness of Blue.
Blue represents peace, trust, and stability. It reminds us of the sky and the ocean, bringing a sense of calm and clarity. This color often symbolizes confidence and inspires a cool, refreshing feeling wherever it appears.


YELLOW

The Brightness of Yellow.
Yellow symbolizes happiness, energy, and positivity. It shines with warmth like the sun, spreading joy and creativity. This color brings life to any space and encourages an optimistic and cheerful mood.


GREEN

The Freshness of Green.
Green represents nature, growth, and harmony. It reminds us of trees, grass, and all living things that bring balance to our world. This color refreshes the mind and symbolizes renewal, hope, and a peaceful life.
